Jehovah's View of Time

1,000 years equal one day in Jehovah's view of time. (Ps 90:4; 2 Pet 3:8)

Therefore, in his view of time, 41.656 years = 1 hour, and 0.694 years = 1 minute.

  • Jesus' death (33 CE) ≈ 47 hours in Jehovah's view.
  • Adam's creation (4026 BCE) ≈ 6 days + 1 hour (as of 2025).
